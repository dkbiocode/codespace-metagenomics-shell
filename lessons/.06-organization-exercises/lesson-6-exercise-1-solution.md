> ## Exercise 1: Making and organized file system   
> Use the `mkdir` command to make the following directories:   
> - `workshop`
> - `workshop/docs`
> - `workshop/data`
> - `workshop/results`
>

> ## Solution
>
> ~~~
> $ mkdir workshop
> $ mkdir workshop/docs
> $ mkdir workshop/data
> $ mkdir workshop/results
> ~~~
